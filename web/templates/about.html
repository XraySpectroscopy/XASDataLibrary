Welcome to the XAS Spectral Library.

This is a collection of X-ray Absorption Spectra. Generally, the data here are intended to be of high quality and on well-characterized samples, but no guarantees are made about either of these.

Disclaimer:
All data files extracted from this library have been put in the Public Domain, and can be used for any purpose, without restrictions. To better explain the data, information is included about the beamlines and facilities (typically National Labs) where the data were collected, and sometimes the people involved with the measurement. This should not be viewed as an endorsement of the data, or as a notice of copyright. No additional permission is needed to use any of the data in this library.

XDI -- the XAFS Data Interchange Format.

To import data into this library, the data must be in the XAS-Data-Interchange (XDI) format, and must have a file extenstion of '.xdi'.

A detailed description of this format can be found at XAS Data Interchange Specification. Example files are at Example XDI data and in this collection.

Suites -- Tagging Related Spectra

A suite is a collection of similar spectra.

Rating Spectra and Suites

Either individual spectra or suites of spectra can be rated and reviewed by any one who has logged in. Scores can range from 0 to 5 (similar to many on-line shopping sites), and comments on the spectrum or suite can be made.

Uploading Data
